Welcome to the updated Unconverted Trading Guide.<p>

As of <b>May 1, 2021</b>, the guide is being hosted and updated by <a href="http://www.neopets.com/neomessages.phtml?type=send&recipient=superkathiee">Kat</a>. Mike, the previous owner, is now <i>free</i> from Neopets. We love you, Meatcake!

</p><p>This is a guide built for the PC which has been handed down to different users over the years. The guide's main focus is to keep structure to pet trading on the PC. It's a 'capitalist' version of the values of unconverted pets.</p><p>

<b>This trading guide is not law.</b> It is merely a guide to help determine general pet values. Individual pet values may fluctuate based on personal taste.</p><p>

<b>IT IS EXTREMELY IMPORTANT TO NOTE</b> that as of


</p><p>

This guide works in assuming pets traded have a VWN name format. As mentioned above, these are NOT law, but observations on how common pets are and how they fluctuate based on how easy/difficult it can be to find them.</p><div style="clear: left;"></div>

<center>

<div id="guide" style="vertical-align: top;"><img src="http://i.imgur.com/pTrd7S4.png"><p>

As experienced traders will tell you, names can play a BIG factor in how well a pet trades! Because of this, the values can and will fluctuate. As per most trading guides, this guide is built assuming all pets have equivalent names. A VWN lower tiered pet could easily trade above their tier, and vice versa with a BN higher tiered pet. Occasionally, you may see users over-offering for a specific pet. Those making conscious over-offers will not make a pet go up in value, so please be wary! The best possible advice you can take is to never make a trade you are uncomfortable with! Don't trade for a pet you would be upset with keeping.

</p><p>

<b>VWN:</b> Roughly 8 or so letters | Capitalized | Visually Appealing | Pronounceable

<br><b>WN:</b> 8+ Letters | Visually Appealing |Pronounceable

<br><b>DN:</b> Compound Names OR 1-2 Underscores OR 1 Number | Relatively Pronounceable

<br><b>BN:</b> More Than 1 Number OR 1-2 Underscores OR Unpronounceable

<br><b>VBN:</b> Multiple Numbers | Multiple Underscores OR Extremely Long | Visually Unappealing</p><p>
